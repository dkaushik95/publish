Role: Stakeholder
This role represents interest groups whose needs must be satisfied by the project. It is a role that may be played by anyone who is (or potentially will be) materially affected by the outcome of the project.

    Main Description
    This role will be providing all the information related to the project. What are his needs, his goals, how the product will function and other such related informaiton will be given by him. In this phase he/she will decide if he/she satisfied with the final product or not, and decide in what invironment the product will be deployed.
